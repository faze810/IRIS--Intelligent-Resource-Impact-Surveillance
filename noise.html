Health Impacts of Noise Pollution
😴
Sleep Disruption
Noise above 40 dB at night disrupts REM sleep cycles, affecting cognitive function and immunity.
❤️
Cardiovascular Stress
Chronic noise exposure raises blood pressure and increases heart disease risk by up to 20%.
👂
Hearing Loss
Prolonged exposure to 85+ dB causes irreversible noise-induced hearing loss (NIHL).
🧠
Cognitive Impairment
Children in noisy environments show impaired reading, memory, and concentration.

Did You Know?
India's major cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai) consistently exceed WHO noise guidelines. The WHO recommends outdoor ambient noise below 53 dB for good health outcomes.
